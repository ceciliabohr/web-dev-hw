About my Instructable


Why did I choose this project?

I chose latte art for my project because I used to be a barista for almost five years, and I loved teaching new hires how to do latte art!

What does it mean for you?

I have recently moved on from working as a barista, and I miss it everyday. source when I get the chance to talk or show people what I used to do on daily basis, I love to show them how it works. Not many people know how latte art works so showing them how to do it is always exciting!
