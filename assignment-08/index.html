How To Create Latte Art in Five Steps!


What You Will Need:

  • 1 shot of espresso
  • 4-5 ounces of whole milk
  • a stainless steel pitcher
  • a steam wand to steam milk
  • a 12-16 ounce mug


Step One:

Steam milk until it's thick and creamy, with not too much foam.

Step Two:

Pour espresso into mug, keeping it to one side of the mug.

Step Three:

Pour steamed milk from pitcher into the espresso, slowly at first, then gradually speeding up.

Step Four:

When the milk is halfway gone, keep pouring while gently moving the pitcher right to left, to facilitate the foam pour.

Step Five:

When the milk is almost all in the mug, and the "apple" shape appears, reduce pouring speed and move the pitcher over and across the surface. The small remaining trail of foam will pull a line through the center of the "apple" shape, creating a heart shape. And voila!
