plant haven

Plants are mainly multicellular, predominantly photosynthtic eukaryotes of the kingdom Plantae. Historicaly, plants were treated as one of two kingdoms including all living things that were not animals, and all algae and fungi were treated as plants. However, all current definitions of Plantae exclude the fungi and some algae, as well as the prokaryotes. There are over 391,000 different place species in this world.
